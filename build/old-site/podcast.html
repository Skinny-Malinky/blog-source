Podcast!

Podcast! is a project driven by my love for podcasts. There’s a lack of web services specifically made for listening to podcasts, so I made my own while teaching myself how to use javascript and jQuery.

It will take any podcast RSS feed and appropriate it into the service’s format. I plan to develop the app further to give people personal feeds, so that they’re able to organise their own roster of podcasts.

You can try it out here: http://www.struanfraser.co.uk/beta

And you can follow my progress here: http://www.struanfraser.co.uk/blog
