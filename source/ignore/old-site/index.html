Hello!

I'm Struan. I'm a Scottish Interaction Designer that specialises in prototyping. I love experimenting with new technologies, playing with silly ideas and creating fun, beautiful experiences.

The work here's a little out of date but I plan on updating it lickety-split.

Cropshare

Cropshare allows allotment goers to sell their excess produce to people who live nearby.

It's a small step to help promote food sovereignty and provenance.

An' A That

An' A That is an Android tablet app for playing with and re-arranging the works of Rabbie Burns in a playful and social way.

High Five O Meter

The High Five O Meter rates the strength of your high five and posts the results to Twitter with a colloquial message.

DIxD Dictionary

The DIxD Dictionary is an iPad app that offers new students a dictionary of terms relating to IxD.

Oxjam Dundee

2012 Oxjam Dundee's posters, flyers and tickets. The event was a huge success.

Twittering

A Twitter activated phone that keeps students and parents in touch.

Barnbrook Gallery
